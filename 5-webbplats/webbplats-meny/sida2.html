Molchat Doma

Molchat Doma (Russian: Молчат Дома IPA: [mɐlˈt͡ɕat dɐˈma];[1] English: "Houses Are Silent")[2] is a Belarusian rock band from Minsk, formed in 2017.

Their current lineup consists of Egor Shkutko (vocals), Roman Komogortsev (guitar, synthesizer, drum machine), and Pavel Kozlov (bass guitar, synthesizer).

Their sound was influenced by 1980s Russian rock music and has been described as post-punk, new wave, synth-pop, and cold wave.
